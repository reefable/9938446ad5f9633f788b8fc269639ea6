    Recruitment, Screening, Selection

    Our recruitment process is dedicated to providing crews with maximum expertise and self-sustainable capability. We hold an extensive database of qualified and experienced Indonesian seafarers. We also emphasize that our crewmembers should be able to communicate effectively in English. Our up-to-date computerized database empowered with a search solutions which help us to find the suitable candidates quickly. All candidates are screened, qualifications and experiences verified and cross-checked, furthermore a personal interview has been conducted. In case our Principal wishes to screen selected candidates personally we welcome Principal’s representatives to interview them.

    Read More

    Documents, Training, Certificates

    Our seafarers meet the very highest technical and operational standards. We conduct thorough verification of candidate’s documents and certifications, to ensure that this qualification, experience and documents in order to suit the Principal's strict standard demands. Training and certificates comply with IMO, STCW, ISM, ISPS and Flag State requirements and always have the necessary experience relevant to the type of vessels for which the seafarers have been hired. Requirements of the Vessel's Flag Registry administration, crews would be applied the appropriate flag licenses and corresponding special qualifications or statutory certificates.

    Read More

    Dispatching and Travel Arrangement

    When the seafarers are suitable and approved by Principal, we communicate with the Principal to arrange the necessary documents such as Flag State License (depending on the flag of the vessel), Letter of Guarantee or / and OK to Board, Article of Agreement, Visa, Air Ticket, and any other requirement in order that the crew can go from place of origin to country or vessel of destination without hindrance. As soon as we received the documents required, we will deploy the seafarers as soon as possible to meet the vessels and to avoid delay. Then we liaison with local agents regarding flight schedule and other necessary details to complete pre-arrival formalities with the immigration authorities, of subject vessel, at the joining port in order that the crews are able to join ship in enough time for a proper handing/taking over.
